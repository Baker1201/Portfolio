Creative Projects

ICWS Creative Writing Anthology

I was the Editor of the ICWS Creative Writing Anthology for three years during my university studies. I worked with a diverse group of student writers to curate and develop a range of creative pieces, from fiction to poetry. This experience helped me cultivate a collaborative editorial style and a deep appreciation for literary creativity.
