Fiction Editing Services

I offer a range of fiction editing services tailored to meet your manuscript’s needs, whether you're at the beginning of your writing journey or ready for final polish.

  • Developmental Editing: A big-picture look at your manuscript, focusing on plot structure, character development, pacing, and narrative flow.
  • Line Editing: Fine-tuning your writing by focusing on sentence structure, tone, and flow, ensuring that your language shines and the narrative voice is clear and engaging.
  • Copyediting: Correcting grammar, punctuation, spelling, and consistency, ensuring your manuscript is polished and error-free.
  • Manuscript Evaluation: A comprehensive evaluation of your manuscript with detailed feedback on its strengths and areas for improvement.
